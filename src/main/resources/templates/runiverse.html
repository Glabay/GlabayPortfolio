Welcome to The Runiverse

The Runiverse is a set of Discord Bots designed to complete the integration between RSPS and Discord. We help you eliminate the need for a website by leveraging Discord's community and forum features. Let us connect your Discord with your RSPS, allowing your players to do everything they can on a website within Discord itself.

Simply invite the core Runiverse bot to your server, use /register with it, tell us who you are, and provide a link to your site and launcher/client. We will generate a RuneVote listing, and from there, you can invite the bots you want.

Runiverse:

One single place for all your RSPS needs, from Server-Owners to Server-Players, or just a curious adventurer looking for that next place to call home. With a collection of services from voting to shopping, and with ways to view the leaderboards, there is no place like ::1.

With this bot, Owners can register their Discord Guild with their RSPS. From here you can implement our free services and much more to come.

RuneVote:

RuneVote is your all-in-one solution for seamlessly integrating top-list advertisements and a user-friendly rewards store directly into your Discord server. Designed with gamers and server owners in mind, RuneVote leverages microservices and a powerful Discord bot interface to streamline the process of incentivizing votes and offering enticing rewards, all without the hassle of leaving the comfort of your Discord community.

RuneScore:

Bringing e-commerce to Discord. Simply create your store, add your stock, and wait for players to view your store and buy your products! The RuneStore checkout is compatible with PayPal only, so you will need a PayPal account.
